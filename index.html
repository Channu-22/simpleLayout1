Vitthal Temple

This image showcases the famous Stone Chariot at the Vijaya Vittala Temple in Hampi, Karnataka. Hampi, once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The Stone Chariot, made of granite, resembles a wooden chariot and is known for its intricate carvings. Behind it, the temple ruins and a partially collapsed gopuram add to its historical significance. The temple complex also features musical pillars that produce sounds when tapped. Hampi remains a major tourist attraction, reflecting the grandeur of medieval South Indian architecture.
